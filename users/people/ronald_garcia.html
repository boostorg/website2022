--- title: Ronald Garcia copyright: Rene Rivera 2004-2005. revised: --- Ronald Garcia Ronald Garcia ============= ![-](ronald_garcia.jpg) Ronald Garcia is a researcher at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA. He is the author of the Boost Multidimensional Array Library ([MultiArray](/doc/libs/release/libs/multi_array/doc/index.html)). His research interests include software engineering tools and techniques, programming languages, generic and generative programming and high performance scientific computing.
