--- title: Community copyright: Rene Rivera 2007. revised: --- Community /\*\*/ Community ========= Although Boost was begun by members of the C++ Standards Committee Library Working Group, participation has expanded to include thousands of programmers from the C++ community at large. If you are interested in participating in Boost, please join our main [developers mailing list](groups.html#main). Discussions are highly technical, and listed members are encouraged to participate in formal reviews of proposed libraries. There is also a [users mailing list](groups.html#users), and several [project specific lists](groups.html#projects). If you are looking for more lively interaction with the Boost community there are also some [internet relay chat](irc.html) channels (freenode) that users and developers like to hang out in.
