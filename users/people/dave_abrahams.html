Rene Rivera 2004-2005. revised: --- Dave Abrahams Dave Abrahams ============= ![-](dave_abrahams.jpg) [Dave Abrahams](mailto:dave@boost-consulting.com) is a founding member and moderator of Boost, and an active member of the wider open-source community. He has been an ANSI/ISO C++ committee member since 1996, and has worked in the software industry since 1988. In 2001 He founded [Boost Consulting](http://www.boostpro.com) (now Boostpro Computing) , a company dedicated to providing professional support and development services for the Boost C++ libraries and associated tools. Dave often shows up at C++ standards committee meetings on a bicycle. He lives in Somerville, Massachusetts with his family.
