--- title: Mark Rodgers copyright: Rene Rivera 2004-2005. revised: --- Mark Rodgers Mark Rodgers ============ ![-](mark_rodgers.jpg) Mark Rodgers lives in [Wellington](http://www.wellington.govt.nz/), the capital of New Zealand, with his wife, Clare, and their son, Ronnie. He studied Computer Science at [Victoria University of Wellington](http://www.vuw.ac.nz/home/default.aspx) from 1983 to 1986, completing a B.Sc. (Hons). He now works as consultant through his company, Cadenza New Zealand Ltd, and also markets Cadenza Drawing Board™, a CAD system he developed. Mark has been programming in C++ since about 1990, and loves every minute of it, but is continually amazed at how much more he still has to learn.
